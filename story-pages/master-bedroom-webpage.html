You push open the door to the master bedroom. The room is in disarray, the bed unmade and clothes scattered everywhere.

The air in the room is thick with tension and the remnants of long-fought battles. The wallpaper is torn and the bedspread is stained, hinting at the turmoil that has taken place here. A vanity table is overturned, and the shards of a broken mirror reflect the distorted scene.

Before you can react, you hear a shriek and turn just in time to see an older woman with tattered clothing charging at you, clutching a knife. Her eyes are wild, filled with primal fear and desperation. She lunges at you, and you have no choice but to defend yourself.
