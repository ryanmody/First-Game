You search for something, anything, to defend yourself. and decayed, lunges at you, hurling glassware with inhuman force. Cobwebs stretch across the corners of the ceiling, and flies buzz around the overflowing trash can. Each step you take crunches on shards of broken glass, amplifying the eerie silence. As you rummage through the drawers, you hear a piercing screech behind you. You turn around, and see a ghastly figure, twisted - standing between you and the way back to the living room.
