As you ascend the final step to the upstairs hallway, your breath comes in ragged gasps. Each creaking step, each crack in the plaster, seems to accuse you silently. The hallway comes into view.

She's the reason, you know. She let the house fall into neglect, garbage and rot piling in every corner of the room, furniture falling apart. The incident with the knife just a short while ago, left a scar etched deep into your flesh, still throbs as harsh reminder. And now this ungodly creature is hunting you relentlessly. It's her fault - she's brought this upon us both.

The master bedroom door is open just ahead of you.
