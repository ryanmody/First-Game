The wooden floorboards creak under your weight, and the faded wallpaper is marked with stains and scratches. The photos on the wall, though their details are obscured, evoke a strange sense of deja vu. A faint, almost imperceptible scent of perfume lingers in the air, mingling with the musty odor of the house. The soft, muffled crying from the room at the end of the hall sends chills down your spine.

You can finally tell where the muffled noises are coming from - at the end of the hallway, there's a wooden door that's closed shut. You slowly creep up until you make your way to the door.
